Sandhill Cranes: Itinerant Ravenous Marauders - they kill at-will

Sandhill cranes are eat pretty much anything - with a diet heavy in seeds and cultivated grains, but may also include berries, tubers ( potatoes ), small vertebrates, and invertebrates. Nonmigratory populations eat adult and larval insects, snails, reptiles, amphibians, nestling birds, small mammals, seeds, and berries. As migratory birds, cranes will move south and east during the winter as food becomes scarce.
